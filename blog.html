5 ways artificial intelligence is assisting college students in their daily lives in India.

:- Shivraj Patil

Introduction

AI is assisting college students in their daily lives in India by providing a variety of benefits. It has made learning more enjoyable and interactive for the students. .In India, education is considered to be one of the pillars of a country's economy. It provides citizens with an opportunity to make a living in their respective fields and help the economy prosper. AI has been implemented into education with the help of machine learning.The benefits that AI has provided for students in India are efficiency, accuracy, and speed.

1) Finding jobs and internships based on their qualifications and skillsets.

AI has helped to shrink the so-called “skills gap,” where there are not enough skilled workers to fill future job openings. With AI, employers can now analyze and match potential employees with the appropriate positions more efficiently.AI has helped students find more than 3,400 jobs in sectors like retail, finance and technology.

Examples of ai based job and internship portals:-

2) epicareer.com

2) Use of ai chatbots in course management system.

AI chatbots are the new way of increasing engagement with the college courses. These chatbots are programmed to answer questions and provide feedback on assignments. They can also answer questions about course material and provide tutoring services. The use of AI chatbots in the course management system has been a boon for students. They can now interact with their teachers on a one-on-one basis, without having to wait in long queues or attend office hours. Chatbots can be a great way for colleges to reach out to their alumni, provide them with updates on the college and answer any questions they might have. Chatbots can also help colleges find potential donors or funders by providing them with information on how they can donate or contribute to the college.
example: superbot-indias first educational chatbot.

3) ai driven guidance and support system for students.

AI is a great driving force for students. It has the potential to help students find their strengths and weaknesses, and also provide them with guidance on which classes to take. An AI driven system can be used to provide personalized guidance and support for students. These systems can identify the student’s strengths and weaknesses, as well as suggest courses that they should take in order to improve their skillsets. AI driven guidance systems are a great way of providing support for students who are struggling with college-related decisions. These systems can also help identify a student's strengths and weaknesses when it comes to picking out which classes they should take in order to achieve their goals.

4)Can accsess mental health using NLP.

AI has also led to improvements in clinical assessment for at-risk individuals. One such application is suicide risk assessment based on natural language processing. This involves using NLP to analyze user input in order to identify potential suicide risks. This allows healthcare professionals to provide vulnerable individuals with crisis support and assistance with distress management early in their illness process- creating opportunities for earlier intervention and pathways of care for vulnerable youth.

5) Ai makes learning fun and entertaining for youths.

Ai is quickly becoming one of the most popular technologies among young people. And it's not just because it's new and shiny - ai is actually making learning fun and entertaining for youth. Some of the top ai applications for education include:
- Duolingo: Duolingo is a free language learning app that uses ai to provide a personalized learning experience for each user.
- spellingbee: spellingbee is an AI-powered spelling game that helps kids learn spelling in a fun and engaging way.
- Quizbot: Quizbot is an ai-powered quiz game that helps kids learn about a variety of topics, from animals to history to science. These are just a few examples of how ai is changing the way young people learn. With ai, learning can be more fun, interactive

Conclusion

AI is helping students in India with their studies, personal lives and career. India is a country where the education system has not been able to keep up with the growing demand for skilled labor. The government has been struggling to provide quality education to its citizens. AI can help solve this problem by assisting students with their studies, personal lives and career.
